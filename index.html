Get Voting

Voting is the cornerstone of democracy. It's the most direct way to get involved as an active citizen. The problem is that too many people don't exercise their right to vote, either because they don't know how, can't plan ahead, or even get obstructed. These are some steps to take now.

Register to Vote

Many people don't know you have to register in your area to vote. Find out if you're registered to vote in your area and register online if you're not.

Stay Informed

Another essential part of being a good citizen is being well-informed and educated. If you don't have reliable information, you can't make good decisions and judgements about our government. With all the information around because of social media, traditional media, and your peers, it's hard to know what to listen to. Here's some tips on how to get good information and support the free press.

Review Your Sources

Take a moment to look at how you get news and see if it's reliable. Do you have many sources of information? How do you know they're accurate? What are your own biases?

Buy Your News

Traditional media does a lot of the heavy lifting to get you fact-based information about politics. However, because of the way these media outlets are paid, they sometimes attract sensational stories.
